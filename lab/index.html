Methodology

How everything here is measured.

Show your work, or the numbers mean nothing. Here's exactly how, when and with what.

◇ Bodyweight

Same scale, every Sunday AM, post-bathroom, pre-food, pre-water. The headline figure is a 7-day rolling average — daily weight swings ±1kg on water and food alone, so a single reading is meaningless.

◇ Body composition

Monthly multi-frequency bioimpedance (InBody-class), fasted, same time of day, same hydration state. Treated as a trend line, not gospel — bioimpedance can be off by several percent in absolute terms but is consistent enough to track direction.

◇ Tape & photos

Circumference measured cold at marked sites, average of two readings. Photos shot to a fixed rig: same height, distance, room, light, pose and time of day. The only thing allowed to change is the body.

◇ Strength

Top working sets logged in JSON.fit on the program I'm running. Same lifts, same form standards, same rest. Estimated 1RMs are calculated, not maxed out — I'm here to grow, not to test singles.

This is an n=1 experiment on one person — me. It's a transparent record of what I'm doing and what's happening, not medical, nutrition or training advice. Consumer body-composition tools carry real measurement error; I publish the unflattering numbers (including fat gained on a bulk) precisely because selectively showing only the good ones is how you lose trust. Read it as a logbook, not a prescription.
