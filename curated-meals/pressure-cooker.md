# Curated Meals — Pressure Cooker

1 meal that can be cooked in a pressure cooker. The pressure cooker version is faster than slow cooker (~45 minutes total vs 4 hours) with very similar results.

Method equipment requirements are listed per method. The user must have ALL listed equipment. If they don't, skip the method (try another method of the same meal, or a different meal).
