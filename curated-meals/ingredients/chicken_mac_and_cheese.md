## Notes
- **The high-protein trick:** blend cottage cheese into the cheese sauce — pushes protein to ~80g/serve while staying indulgent. (Roux with butter + flour is the alternative, lower-protein, sauce base.)
- The deliberate **high-calorie comfort/mass main** — for hitting a surplus when appetite is the limiter. First baked/oven dish in the library.
- Default breast; rotisserie/pre-cooked shredded is a valid shortcut. High-protein pasta optional swap.
- Fat (33g) is a touch under a 38 target — 200g cheese is already generous, left honest rather than padded.
- Optional: bacon/pancetta, breadcrumb top, spring onion. Excellent meal-prepper — bakes in a tray, portions cleanly, reheats well 4-5 days in the microwave.
