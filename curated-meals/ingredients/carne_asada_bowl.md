## Notes
- **First whole-cut beef dish** in the library — charred, citrusy contrast to the soy-glaze beef meals (distinct from the mexican chicken meals too).
- **Default: lean sirloin** (~5g fat/100g, accessible). Flank/skirt are the authentic carne asada cuts — more marbled/fattier (~14g fat/100g) ≈ 1041 / 57 / 107 / 41. Cut is the main fat swing.
- Protein runs ~62g (200g lean sirloin is genuinely high-protein) — left honest; good for a bulking main.
- **Marinade timing matters: 2-4 hrs, NOT more than 8** — citrus breaks the steak down and turns it mushy.
- **Slice thin AGAINST the grain** after resting — the key tenderness step.
- Budget-friendly: rice + beans stretch the steak. Keeps 3-4 days; steak best sliced fresh or reheated gently.
- Optional (not in base macros): avocado/guac, jalapeños, sour cream/Greek yoghurt, salsa.
