## ⚠ Not a meal-prep dish
Cook fresh, eat immediately. The egg-cheese emulsion breaks and goes grainy if stored or reheated. Scaled per serving, not as a stored batch.

## Plates
### `standard` — Spaghetti Carbonara
- **Macros:** 850 kcal / 42g P / 92g C / 34g F / 4g fiber
- **Base servings consumed:** 1.0
- **Additional ingredients:** none
- **Note:** No cream. Critical step — pan OFF the heat before the egg goes in, or it scrambles. Loosen with starchy pasta water to a glossy emulsion. Egg = 1 whole + 1 extra yolk per serving. Default bacon/parmesan; guanciale + pecorino are the authentic upgrades. Does NOT keep — eat fresh.
