## Notes
- **Distinct from sheet_pan_salmon_potatoes** — second salmon prep, completely different profile (sweet-savoury Asian glaze + noodles vs Mediterranean tray + potatoes). Films great: glossy glaze + cross-section.
- 200g salmon/serve, 70g dry noodles/serve. Fat (33g) = salmon's natural fat + sesame/neutral oil (verified). Honey is the carb driver.
- Any noodle works (egg/soba/udon/rice); **rice noodles = gluten-free** (drop the Gluten/Wheat allergen if used).
- Keeps 3 days; salmon reheats gently; noodles stored separate ideal.
- Garnish (not in base macros): sesame seeds, spring onion, sliced chilli, optional sriracha for heat.
