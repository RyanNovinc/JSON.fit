## Notes
- **Curry powder is the key** — it's what makes this taste like Aussie-Chinese-restaurant satay, not Thai skewer satay. Don't omit.
- **Default cut: chicken thigh.** Breast swap is leaner: ~805 / 58 / 79 / **29**.
- High-fat by design — peanut butter (80g) + coconut milk (150ml) are both fat-dense; fat stays ~45g even trimmed. This is the library's only peanut-sauce flavour profile.
- Velvet the chicken (soy + cornstarch) for tenderness.
- Keeps/reheats well 3-4 days (peanut sauce reheats better than egg/cream sauces); store rice separate.
- Optional garnish (not in base macros): crushed peanuts, coriander, sliced chilli. Restaurant levers: a little MSG + a very hot pan.
