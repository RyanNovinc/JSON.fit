## Notes
- **First and only lamb dish** — completes the protein spread. Pairs with chicken shawarma (same Mediterranean lane, same garlic-yoghurt sauce).
- **Default: lean lamb mince (~85/15).** Regular (~80/20) is juicier but pushes fat to ~52-60g/serve. Lamb is the fattiest protein in the library — even lean it runs higher-fat than poultry. Beef or lamb-beef mix = milder/cheaper swap.
- **Cinnamon is the key warm note — don't omit.** Grated onion keeps the koftas moist; don't overmix; chill before searing.
- Pita authentic; large tortilla the accessible stand-in. Sumac + chilli flakes optional-if-available.
- Excellent meal-prepper: koftas refrigerate/freeze and reheat well; great cold over salad. Rice/sauce stored separate.
