## Notes
- **Default cut: chicken thigh** (juicier/authentic). Breast is the leaner swap — drops fat materially (rice_bowl with breast ≈ 743 / 63 / 89 / **14**). Fat verified against oil + yoghurt + thigh; the higher fat vs a ~22-24 target is the thigh cut, not estimation.
- 200g chicken raw per serve. Greek yoghurt does double duty (marinade + garlic sauce).
- Pita/flatbread authentic; large tortilla the accessible stand-in. Fries traditionally go in the wrap but omitted from base for lean positioning. Feta/pickles/hot sauce optional, not in base.
- Chicken, rice, sauce keep 3-4 days; assemble fresh.
