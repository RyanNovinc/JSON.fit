## Notes
- **The lowest-effort meal on the list** — chop, toss, roast. Second oven dish; second pork option (via pork sausage).
- **Default: chicken/turkey sausage** (leaner, ~10g fat/100g, better protein-per-cal). **Pork sausage** is the richer option — at the same portions ≈ 940 / 35 / 66 / **61**. Sausage fat % is the single biggest swing on the dish.
- kcal (733) lands a touch under an 820 target because a lean-sausage tray isn't very calorie-dense; pushing it higher means more oil or fattier sausage, both raising fat past 38. Left honest.
- Single layer, don't crowd (crowding steams instead of browns). Stagger: potatoes/onion/peppers/sausage first, broccoli added partway.
- Optional (not in base macros): parmesan or a fried egg on top, chilli flakes. Most sausages contain wheat rusk — gluten-free sausages exist.
- Great meal-prepper: roasts in a batch, keeps/reheats well 4 days, minimal cleanup.
