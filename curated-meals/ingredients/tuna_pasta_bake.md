## Notes
- **The budget hero** — cheapest, most pantry-stable meal on the list; no fresh shopping needed (peas/corn frozen or canned). Second fish in the library.
- **High-protein trick:** blended cottage cheese + milk + cheddar sauce base (like the mac and cheese) instead of a heavy roux — creamy and protein-rich, fat moderate.
- ~370g drained tuna (2 large cans, ~90g/serve); tuna is very lean so protein is high for the calories. Cheese (130g/batch) is the main fat lever, kept modest.
- Broccoli swaps for peas/corn; optional cream cheese for extra creaminess; breadcrumb top optional.
- One of the best meal-preppers: bakes in a tray, keeps/reheats well 4 days (splash of milk/water to loosen).
