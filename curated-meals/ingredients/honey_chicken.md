## Notes
- **Default: breast, pan-fried** (cornstarch-coated, shallow-fried) for meal-prep suitability and lower fat. Deep-fried battered (egg + flour) is the authentic indulgent variant — adds ~10-15g fat/serve.
- Carbs run high (~104g) by design — the honey glaze (90g/batch) plus rice is the defining feature.
- Fat is low (15g) because the lighter pan-fry retains little oil — honest to the lighter default.
- **Reheat honesty flag:** crispy coating softens on reheating — best fresh; glazed chicken still reheats fine for meal prep, just less crunchy. Store rice separate.
- Toss chicken through glaze just before serving to keep it crisper. Garnish: sesame seeds + spring onion.
