## Plates
### `standard` — Chicken Fajita Rice Bowl
- **Macros:** 760 kcal / 52g P / 92g C / 14g F / 10g fiber
- **Base servings consumed:** 1.0
- **Additional ingredients:** none
- **Note:** Fajita blend = cumin + chilli powder + paprika + garlic powder + salt + pepper (store-bought packet is an equivalent shortcut). 200g chicken/portion; rice + black beans for carbs & fibre. One of the best meal-preppers — store components separately, keeps 4-5 days, reheat rice with a splash of water. Fresh toppings (avocado, sour cream, cheese, coriander) added at serving, NOT in base macros. Variants: cauliflower-rice (lower carb) or tortilla wrap.
