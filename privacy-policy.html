Privacy Policy

🔒 Your data stays on your device. Always.
Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection Overview

JSON.fit is a comprehensive fitness and nutrition app that prioritises your privacy by storing all personal data locally on your device. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your workout routines, meal plans, health data, or personal information to external servers.

🛡️

Local Storage Only: All your workouts, nutrition plans, health information, and personal data remain on your device and never leave it.

This includes sensitive health information such as dietary restrictions, medical conditions, weight tracking, and nutritional goals — all stored securely on your device only.

2. What Data We Store Locally

The following information is stored exclusively on your device:

Fitness & Workout Data:

  • Imported workout routines
  • Exercise progress and logs
  • Workout calendar data
  • Personal notes and customisations

Nutrition & Health Data:

  • Personal nutrition goals and macro targets
  • Health conditions and dietary restrictions
  • Weight tracking history and measurements
  • Meal plans, recipes, and cooking instructions
  • Food inventory and grocery shopping lists
  • Budget information and location for pricing
  • Supplement intake and nutrient deficiency tracking
  • Meal timing schedules and food preferences
  • Meal prep sessions and cooking instructions

General App Data:

  • App preferences and settings
  • Purchase history for Pro Access features

3. What We Don't Collect

We do not collect, process, or store:

  • Personal identifying information
  • Workout performance data
  • Device location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking
  • Social media or contact information

4. Health Data and Nutrition Information

Our app collects and processes sensitive health and nutrition information to provide personalised meal planning and dietary guidance. This includes:

🛡️

Health Data Sensitivity: We treat your health information with the highest level of privacy protection by keeping it exclusively on your device.

  • Medical conditions that affect nutrition (diabetes, hypertension, etc.)
  • Known nutrient deficiencies and supplementation
  • Pregnancy, breastfeeding, or other life stage considerations
  • Digestive issues and food absorption concerns
  • Energy levels and metabolic patterns

Important: This app is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult healthcare providers for medical nutrition therapy or if you have serious health conditions.

5. App Store Purchases

When you purchase Pro Access through the App Store, Apple handles all payment processing. We receive only basic purchase confirmation to unlock premium features. No payment details are stored by our app.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your information security depends on your device's security measures:

  • Use device passcode/biometric locks
  • Keep your device's OS updated
  • Regular device backups protect your data

7. Data Backup and Sync

Your workout data may be included in iOS device backups (iCloud or iTunes). This backup process is managed entirely by Apple according to their privacy policies. We do not have access to your backup data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally, parental supervision of device usage is recommended for younger users.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be communicated through app updates. Since we don't collect personal data, policy changes typically involve clarifications rather than new data practices.

10. Contact Information

Questions about our privacy practices? Contact us at Ryan.Novinc@gmail.com or through the App Store. Since we don't store personal data, most privacy concerns can be resolved through local device management.
