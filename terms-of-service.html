1. About JSON.fit

JSON.fit is a fitness and nutrition app, built and operated by Ryan Novinc (a sole trader based in Australia), that helps you build and track training programs and meal plans. It works on a "bring your own AI" model: you answer a few questions, the app generates a prompt, you paste that prompt into an AI service of your choice (such as ChatGPT, Claude or Gemini), and you import the result back into the app to track it. The app lets you:

  • Import, manage and track workout routines and training programs
  • Build personalised meal plans and track macros
  • Plan health-conscious meals around dietary restrictions
  • Generate grocery lists with local pricing estimates
  • Track weight and follow meal-prep and cooking guidance
