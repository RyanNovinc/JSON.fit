FAQ

Common questions.

How does it actually work?
You answer a few questions and the app builds a starter prompt. Paste it into any AI you already use, like ChatGPT, Claude or Gemini. From there the AI guides you: it builds your plan, then offers to review it and check the numbers, and once you're happy it converts everything into a file JSON.fit can read. You just confirm each step, then import the file and your plan loads, ready to track.
Do I need to pay for AI separately?
Most AI chatbots have a free tier that works perfectly. ChatGPT, Gemini and others all offer free access. If you're already paying for a premium AI subscription, even better. Either way, JSON.fit itself has no AI costs to pass on to you.
How much does JSON.fit cost?
It's completely free. Workouts and nutrition are all free: meal plans, macro tracking, grocery lists, meal prep and pantry intelligence. No subscription, no paywall, no in-app purchases.
If it's free, what's the catch?
There isn't one. Most fitness apps charge $10–20/month because they run AI on their own servers. JSON.fit uses the AI you already have, so there are no server costs to pass on. There are no accounts and no data collection either, so there's nothing to sell. The app is simply free.
What AI does it work with?
Any AI that can generate structured text, like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and DeepSeek. You're not locked into one provider. Use whatever you prefer.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your data stays on your device. JSON.fit doesn't have accounts, doesn't collect personal information, and doesn't send your workout or nutrition data anywhere.
Is it available on Android?
Not yet. It's currently iOS only, but Android is in development.
