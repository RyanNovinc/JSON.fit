FAQ

Common questions.

How does it actually work?
The app builds a structured prompt based on your goals, schedule, and preferences. You copy it into any AI you already use — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, whatever. The AI generates a personalised plan. You paste the result back into JSON.fit and it loads instantly, ready to track.
Do I need to pay for AI separately?
Most AI chatbots have a free tier that works perfectly — ChatGPT, Gemini, and others all offer free access. If you're already paying for a premium AI subscription, even better. Either way, JSON.fit itself has no AI costs to pass on to you.
How much does JSON.fit cost?
Workouts are free forever — no paywall, no limits. Nutrition features (meal plans, macro tracking, grocery lists, meal prep, pantry intelligence) are a one-time $9.99 USD purchase. No subscription. You pay once and own it forever.
Why is it so much cheaper than other apps?
Most fitness apps charge $10–20/month because they run AI on their servers. JSON.fit uses the AI you already have, so there are no server costs to pass on to you. That's why workouts are free and nutrition is a one-time purchase.
What AI does it work with?
Any AI that can generate structured text — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, and more. You're not locked into one provider. Use whatever you prefer.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your data stays on your device. JSON.fit doesn't have accounts, doesn't collect personal information, and doesn't send your workout or nutrition data anywhere.
Is it available on Android?
Not yet — it's currently iOS only. Android is in development. You can tap "I want it on Android" on this site to let us know there's demand.
